Membership Services
CSI provides a comprehensive bouquet of online services for its members.
Some of the highlights are:

1. Join CSI
2. Renew your membership online
3. Subscribe to CSI Publications
4. Update your profile online
5. Select/change your chapter affiliation
6. Convert your membership category
7. Upgrade your membership grades
8. Post your services offerings on CSI portal

Following are a few other services/benefits CSI provides to its members.

9. It is proposed to give a free email address yourname@csi-india.org
10. Free digital copies of CSI Communications & newsletter
11. A ‘for member only' portlet which can be accessed by CSI members after signing in with your user Id and password.
12. Financial support for research projects

About Membership

The membership categories are:
1. Individual Domestic regular
2. Individual Domestic life
3. Individual International regular
4. Individual International life
5. Student Domestic
6. Educational Institutes Domestic
7. Educational Institutes International
8. All other Institutes Domestic
9. All other Institutes International
10. Subscribers Domestic
11. Subscriber International
12. Student International category is new consideration
The Institutes can nominate their staff member as Nominee members.
CSI also provides complimentary memberships to select professionals and awards winners.

Membership Benefits

Institutional membership is a necessity for starting and maintaining a student Branch. Additionally at least a strength of 75 student members is to be maintained at all times.

Institution Membership

• Recognition by Accreditation bodies for association with a professional organization
• Grant of Free nominee membership to three faculty members with voting rights & log-in privilege on CSI knowledge portal
• 4 copies of CSI Communications every month
• Access to CSI knowledge portal and distinguished speaker list and eligibility for volunteering to be a speaker
• Eligibility to apply for assistance to attend International conferences
• Concessional rates for CSI Conferences and Tutorials for nominees
• Eligibility to publish articles in CSI communications and Transactions for nominees
• Discounted IEEE membership rate for faculty
• Reciprocal membership of BCS, ACS and subsidized product offerings by them
• Benefits available through CSI-IFIP, C-DAC, PMI & Microsoft collaboration

Student Branches (All Advantages of IM Plus)
• Eligibility for nomination of best Student Branch award
• Additional 4 copies of CSI Communications to the institution & log-in privilege to the students on CSI knowledge portal with access to distinguished speaker list
• Attachment to a Regional Student coordinator & National Student coordinator for assistance with resource persons for various events and chapter support to organize seminars / workshops / tutorials / competitions / Expo
• Invitation for all Regional/State/National student conventions/conferences and competitions
• Concessional rates for CSI Conferences and Tutorials for student members
• Opportunity for students to interact with Industry professionals and chalk out a career path
• Eligibility to publish articles in CSI communications and journal for student members
• Eligibility to be nominated for the best Student paper in CSI communications
• Eligibility for CSI funded minor projects (for College Students)
• CSI Certification and Training programs at discounted rates for students
• Permission to conduct events under CSI Banner

Individual Membership
• Access to CSI Knowledge Portal with a unique login ID and Password.
• Opportunity for member to member virtual networking through CSI Communities, Forums, Blogs.
• Both print and electronic versions of CSI-Communications each month.
• Right to vote in CSI elections as well as contest elections subject to the eligibility criteria.
• Concessional registration fee for CSI events and training programs
• Opportunity to offer workshops/ trainings on profit sharing basis in collaboration with CSI.
• Opportunity to deliver guest lectures in educational institutes associated to CSI across India.
• Opportunities to benefit from CSI affiliations with sister societies and collaborating organisations such as Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers, Inc. (IEEE), Project Management Institute (PMI), C-DAC, Singapore Computer Society (SCS), Empowering Women in IT (EWIT), The Institution of Engineers (India) and Microsoft Corporation (India) Private Limited
• Opportunity to become a member of CSI Special Interest Groups and CSI Divisions.
• Opportunity to participate in CSI Divisional research projects.
• Financial support for minor research projects.
• Part sponsorship for presenting papers at prestigious International events

SASTRA University

Computer Society of India established in the year 1965 is today the largest IT professionals Society in India. The purposes of the Society are scientific and educational directed towards the advancement of the theory and practice of computer science, computer engineering and technology, systems science and engineering, information processing and related arts and sciences.
