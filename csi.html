Computer Society of India

Computer Society of India is the first and largest body of computer professionals in India. It was started on 6 March 1965 by a few computer professionals and has now grown to be the national body representing computer professionals. It has 71 chapters across India, 418 student branches, and more than 90,000 members.

Keeping in mind the interest of the IT professionals & computer users, CSI works towards, making the profession an area of choice amongst all sections of the society. The promotion of Information Technology as a profession is the top priority of CSI today. To fulfill this objective, the CSI regularly organizes conferences, conventions, lectures and gives various awards. And at the same time it also ensures that regular training and skill updating are organised for the IT professionals. CSI also works towards a global approach, by seeking out alliances with organizations overseas who may be willing to come forward and participate in such activities. CSI also helps governments in formulating IT strategy & planning.

CSI is one of the most famous society associated with the computer professionals. It was being introduced in the SASTRA University on 5th December 2006 with the institutional membership, with 57 student members.However due to various advancement, today it had grown into an successful society with nearly 252 members. As active participants of this society we conduct monthly students meet where we discuss about the various advancements in technology. In the year 2010 during the month of march we organized the National conference on Information Science and Security in association with computer society of india which proved to be a great success.
